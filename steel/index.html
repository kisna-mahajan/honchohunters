Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Is Honcho Hunters a steel industry jobs portal?

No. Honcho Hunters is a confidential executive search and leadership intelligence platform, not a public job board. We do not list open positions or publish candidate profiles. Organisations share specific leadership mandates, and we identify and introduce relevant leaders directly.

How can I be considered for steel industry leadership roles?

Senior professionals can confidentially share their profile at honchohunters.com/engage/. Profiles are shared only against relevant mandates for CEO/CXO, plant leadership, business transformation and board-level roles.

What leadership roles does Honcho Hunters cover in the steel industry?

Our practice covers leaders driving scale, efficiency and transformation across manufacturing, technical and commercial leadership in steel and metals organisations.

How does the confidential search process work for steel and metals organisations?

Organisations share a leadership mandate with Honcho Hunters. We map the relevant sector leadership landscape, identify relevant executives, confidentially engage them, conduct leadership evaluation, and make an introduction.

Is my profile kept confidential?

Yes. Honcho Hunters does not operate as a public CV marketplace. Profiles are handled discreetly and shared only with directly relevant organisations.
