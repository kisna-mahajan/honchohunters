Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Is Honcho Hunters a healthcare jobs portal?

No. Honcho Hunters is a confidential executive search and leadership intelligence platform, not a public job board. We do not list open healthcare jobs or publish candidate profiles. Hospitals and healthcare organisations share specific leadership mandates, and we identify and introduce relevant leaders directly.

How can a healthcare leader be considered for leadership roles?

Senior healthcare professionals can confidentially share their profile at honchohunters.com/engage/. Profiles are shared only against relevant mandates for roles such as Hospital CEO, Managing Director, Medical Director, clinical operations head or digital health leader.

What healthcare leadership roles does Honcho Hunters cover?

Our healthcare practice covers leaders for hospitals, healthcare enterprises and health-tech organisations, including CEO/CXO, hospital leadership, clinical operations and digital health leadership roles.

How does the confidential search process work for hospitals and healthcare organisations?

Hospitals and healthcare organisations share a leadership mandate with Honcho Hunters. We map the relevant healthcare leadership landscape, identify and confidentially engage suitable leaders, conduct executive evaluation, and make an introduction.

Is my profile kept confidential?

Yes. Honcho Hunters does not operate as a public CV marketplace. Profiles are handled discreetly and shared only with directly relevant organisations.
