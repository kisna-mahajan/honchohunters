Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Who founded Honcho Hunters?

Honcho Hunters is founded on the leadership and consulting legacy of Anil Mahajane, an IIFT-trained management graduate (MBA, 1984) with over 30 years of experience leading, restructuring and scaling businesses across diverse industries.

What is C-Suite Talent Management Consulting?

C-Suite Talent Management Consulting is the parent leadership advisory and executive search practice behind Honcho Hunters. Honcho Hunters extends that practice into six focused, sector-specialised verticals.

How is Honcho Hunters different from a typical recruitment firm?

Honcho Hunters is led by an operator, not a recruiter. Candidates are evaluated on leadership maturity and strategic fit the way a CEO would assess them, not matched against a database by keywords.

Does Honcho Hunters publish candidate profiles publicly?

No. Honcho Hunters does not operate as a public CV marketplace. All engagement is confidential, and profiles are shared only against relevant, mandate-specific opportunities.
