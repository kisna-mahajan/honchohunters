About Honcho Hunters

Leadership Intelligence, Built on Three Decades of Boardroom Experience

Honcho Hunters is a confidential executive search and leadership intelligence platform — a C-Suite Talent Management Consulting initiative — built by practitioners who have led, transformed and scaled businesses themselves, not by recruiters reading résumés.

Our Founder

Leadership Search, Led by a CEO — Not a Recruiter

Honcho Hunters is founded on the leadership and consulting legacy of Anil Mahajane, an IIFT-trained management graduate (MBA, 1984) with over 30 years of experience leading, restructuring and scaling businesses across diverse industries.

Having held CEO and senior leadership roles, and led several business turnarounds, Anil brings an operator's understanding of what makes leadership succeed or fail — an understanding that conventional CV-matching recruitment cannot replicate.

This experience forms the foundation of Honcho Hunters' core philosophy: that identifying the right leader requires industry depth, leadership maturity and an honest read of strategic fit — not keyword matching against a database of profiles.

Our Foundation
