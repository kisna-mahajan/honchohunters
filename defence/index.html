Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Is Honcho Hunters a defence jobs portal or job board?

No. Honcho Hunters is a confidential executive search and leadership intelligence platform, not a public job board. We do not list open defence leadership jobs or publish candidate profiles. Organisations share specific leadership mandates with us, and we identify and introduce relevant senior professionals directly.

How can a senior defence or aerospace professional be considered for leadership roles?

Senior defence, aerospace and strategic industry professionals can confidentially share their profile at honchohunters.com/engage/. Profiles are shared only against relevant mandates for roles such as CEO, Defence Business Head, Aerospace Leader, Manufacturing Head, Technology Leader or Programme Director.

What defence leadership roles does Honcho Hunters cover?

Our defence practice covers CEOs, defence business heads, aerospace leaders, manufacturing heads, technology leaders, programme directors, government-relations executives, strategy leaders and board advisors across defence manufacturing, aerospace, defence electronics and strategic technology organisations.

How does the confidential search process work for defence organisations?

Defence and aerospace organisations share a leadership mandate with Honcho Hunters. We map the relevant leadership landscape, identify and confidentially engage suitable executives, conduct leadership evaluation, and make an executive introduction, without any public posting or open marketplace exposure.

Is my profile or my organisation's mandate kept confidential?

Yes. Honcho Hunters does not operate as a public CV marketplace or open database. Professional profiles and organisational mandates are handled discreetly and shared only with directly relevant counterparts.
