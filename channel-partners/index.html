Our Partnership Philosophy

Our Performance-Based Partnership Model

We believe in rewarding quality — not quantity. We are interested in receiving carefully shortlisted, domain-specific resumes that genuinely match our client requirements. Simply put: we buy quality resumes, not sourcing methods.

Prompt Payment Process

  • Performance-based partnership
  • ₹500–₹2,000 per approved resume, depending on role and seniority
  • Payment processed within 72 hours of approval
  • Candidate contact details remain masked during initial evaluation
  • Submit only 1–3 carefully shortlisted resumes against each assignment

Bulk, duplicate or generic submissions are discouraged.

Quality Evaluation Process

Every resume is evaluated for:

  • Domain Relevance
  • Functional Suitability
  • Stability
  • Quality of Experience
  • Overall Suitability

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

What is a Honcho Hunters Channel Partner?

A Channel Partner is an independent recruitment professional who sources and submits carefully shortlisted, domain-specific resumes against Honcho Hunters' manufacturing and engineering leadership mandates, and is paid on a performance basis for every approved profile.

Who can become a Channel Partner?

Former recruitment agency professionals, independent recruitment consultants, executive search professionals, retired or semi-retired recruiters, HR & talent acquisition professionals, and recruiters between assignments who understand manufacturing and engineering talent.

How are Channel Partners paid?

Channel Partners earn ₹500–₹2,000 per approved resume, depending on the role and seniority of the position.

Who evaluates submitted resumes?

Every resume is evaluated by the Honcho Hunters executive search team against domain relevance, functional suitability, stability, quality of experience and overall suitability for the assignment.

How do I become a Premier Channel Partner?

Premier Channel Partner status is the highest recognition in the network, awarded solely at Honcho Hunters' discretion to professionals who consistently demonstrate exceptional quality, professionalism, confidentiality and long-term commitment.

Do I need previous recruitment agency experience?

Prior agency or executive search experience is preferred but not mandatory. HR & talent acquisition professionals and independent recruiters who understand manufacturing and engineering talent are also welcome to apply.

Can I work part-time?

Yes. Channel Partners can operate full-time, part-time or as a side income, working independently from home or office.

How quickly are approved resumes paid?

Payment is processed within 72 hours of resume approval.
