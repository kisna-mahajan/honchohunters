Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Is Honcho Hunters a GCC jobs portal?

No. Honcho Hunters is a confidential executive search and leadership intelligence platform, not a public job board. We do not list open GCC jobs or publish candidate profiles. Organisations share specific leadership mandates, and we identify and introduce relevant senior leaders directly.

How can a senior professional be considered for GCC leadership roles?

Senior professionals can confidentially share their profile at honchohunters.com/engage/. Profiles are shared only against relevant mandates for roles such as Centre Head, Country Head, GCC CEO, and senior engineering, product or business-function leadership.

What GCC leadership roles does Honcho Hunters cover?

Our GCC practice covers leaders who launch, scale and mature Global Capability Centres, including Centre Heads, Country Heads, GCC CEOs, and engineering, product and business-function leadership.

How does the confidential search process work for organisations building or scaling a GCC?

Organisations share a leadership mandate with Honcho Hunters. We map the relevant leadership landscape, apply leadership intelligence to identify suitable executives, confidentially engage them, conduct executive evaluation, and make an introduction.

Is my profile or my organisation's mandate kept confidential?

Yes. Honcho Hunters does not operate as a public CV marketplace or open database. Profiles and mandates are handled discreetly and shared only with directly relevant counterparts.
